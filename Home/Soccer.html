Oceania Nepali Sports Meet - Sydney 2018


Venue: Wyatt Park
49 Church Street, Lidcombe NSW 2141
Date & Time: Sat 31 Mar, Sun 01 Apr 0900 - 1700
Parking: On premise
Closest Railway Station: Lidcombe & Auburn
Contact: Bijen 0403 240 642

ONSM Soccer 2018 has been completed successfully. It was a great event. Players from around the Oceania region participated on the tournament. Congratulations to all the winners & runner-ups and sincere thanks to all players & their families, volunteers, sponsors, associate organisations and media partners who made this event a grand success.
