Oceania Nepali Sports Meet - Sydney 2018


Venue: Blacktown Tennis Centre
Corner of Sentry Dr & Stanhope Parkway
Stanhope Garden, NSW 2768
Date & Time: Sat 31 Mar, 01 April 0800 - 1700
Mon 02 April 1200 - 1700
Parking: 4P (Over 4P Collect Permit from Reception)
Contact: Jyoti 0432 210 567
Praveen 0418 504 700

ONSM Tennis 2018 has been completed successfully. It was a great event. Players from around the Oceania region participated on Open Men's Single, Master's 35+ Men's Single, U15 Junior's Single and Men's Open Double. Congratulations to all the winners & runner-ups and sincere thanks to all players & their families, volunteers, sponsors, associate organisations and media partners who made this event a grand success.
