Oceania Nepali Sports Meet - Sydney 2018


Venue: Timbrell Park
Henley Marine Drive, Five Docks, NSW 2046
Date & Time: Mon 02 Apr from 0700 am
Parking: On street
Contact: Laxmi 0431 545 254

ONSM 5KM Run 2018 has been completed successfully. It was a great event. Players from around the Oceania region participated on the tournament. Congratulations to all the winners & runner-ups and sincere thanks to all players & their families, volunteers, sponsors, associate organisations and media partners who made this event a grand success.
