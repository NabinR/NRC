Domino's - Table Tennis

Oceania Nepali Sports Meet - Sydney 2018


Venue: T1/5 Clyde Street Rydalmere, NSW 2116
Date & Time: Sat 31 Mar from 0900 - 1700
Parking: On premise
Closest Railway Station: Rydalmere
Contact: Kuldeep 0402 453 449
Suresh 0431 297 983

ONSM Table Tennis 2018 has been completed successfully. It was a great event. Players from NSW, VIC, QLD, ACT, SA and NZ participated in Men's Open, Men's over 35, Ladies Single, Men's Doubles and Mixed Double. Congratulations to all the winners & runner-ups and sincere thanks to all players & their families, volunteers, sponsors, associate organisations and media partners who made this event a grand success.
