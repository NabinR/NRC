Oceania Nepali Sports Meet - Sydney 2018


Venue: 17 Stanton Street, Seven Hills
Date & Time: Sat 31 Mar 0900 to 1700
Sun 01 Apr 0830 to 1700
Parking: On premise
Contact: Sanjay 0434 681 739
Manoj 0432 798 320

ONSM Badminton 2018 has been completed successfully. It was a great event. Players from around the Oceania region participated on the tournament. Congratulations to all the winners & runner-ups and sincere thanks to all players & their families, volunteers, sponsors, associate organisations and media partners who made this event a grand success.
